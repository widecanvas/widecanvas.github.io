THRILLING CHATAKPUR (4 DAYS 3 NIGHTS)
Day 1: Sealdah- NJP: Take a local or Cab to Sealdah and reach by 21:30
hrs. Board Darjeeling Mail . Its schedule departure time is 22.05 hrs.
Day 2: Schedule arrival time is 08:00 hrs at NJP station. Get freshed at Railway
station and then leave for Chatakpur by cab . Breakfast Enroute. Reached
Chatakpur by 12:30PM. Check in to Home stay or similar and after taking rest
gather for Lunch. Rest of the day is for leisure. At Evening Interactive session with
Everester Debraj Dutta. Evening Snacks will be served during this session. Dinner
@ 8:30 PM.
Day 3: After Breakfast leave for the day for Jungle Trek through beautiful Pine
Trees. Come back by afternoon and have lunch at Home stay or similar. At
Evening Slide show of Everest Climbing. Evening Snacks will be served during this
session. Dinner @ 8:30 PM.
Day 4: After Breakfast leave for the day for Bird's Photography. Come back by
afternoon and have lunch at Home stay or similar. At evening local folk shows
organized by Wide Canvas. At Evening Slide show of Everest Climbing. Evening
Snacks will be served during this session. Dinner @ 8:30 PM.
Day 5: After early breakfast take a tour of the beautiful village. After early Lunch
leave the hotel and take a Cab for NJP. Reached NJP by 19:00 hrs: Board
Darjeeling Mail . Its schedule departure time is 20.00 hrs.
Day 6: NJP-Sealdah : Schedule arrival time is 06:00 hrs at Sealdah. Journey end
here and back with lots of memorable moments.
